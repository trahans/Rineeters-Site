Castle in the Clouds

The Lucknow Estate, was built in 1913 high in the Ossipee Mountain Range by Thomas Gustave Plant. It has been known as the Castle in the Clouds since its opening to the public in 1959.

Revel in the beauty of the views, while taking in the magnificent features Plant had installed in his home. Enjoy a self guided tour as if time stood still in the early 1900s, rooms are still set up as if the owners had just stepped out for the day, and vintage apparel adorns the house in closets.

Search for the innovations of the time - central vacuuming, ammonia brine refrigeration, intercom systems. Admission to the mansion is optional. Rates can be found here.

Directions

455 Old Mountain Road, Moultonborough, NH 03254

However, if you want to take a tour you will need to pay for tour tickets either at the gate or inside the gift shop. In any situation, parking is complimentary.

Things To Do

The Lakes Region is popular for hiking, swimming, boating, camping, fishing, kayaking, golfing, breweries, etc. There are tons of scenic views and places to see!

Just north of the Castle is the infamous White Mountains region! Most of the ski slopes offer summer gondola rides with expansive views of Mount Washington, Tuckermans Ravine, and the Presidential Mountain Range.

You can get a similar view with the historic Mount Washington Cog Railway, Cannon Mountain Aerial Tramway, WildCat Mountain Gondola, Hobo Railroad, and so many more.

The Flume Gorge has many spectacular waterfalls in Franconia Notch State Park.

Visit the Lost River Gorge and Boulder Caves to see glacial caves, boulders, and even more waterfalls.

The Kancamangus Highway is an excellent scenic drive through the mountains with many places to stop along the way.

Stephan and I have spent lots of weekends and anniversaries in the Lakes White Mountains regions. Let us know how long you're staying and we can help you enjoy the area! We especially love waterfalls and short hikes if you'd like a recommendation!
